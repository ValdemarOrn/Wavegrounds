Instructions

Zoom & Pan

You can left click the image and hold down the left mouse button to pan.
Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out. You can also use the settings in the editor to zoom and pan

Settings

Hold down the Shift key to fine-tune the settings.
Some settings have an exponential scale, a small change in value may cause a large change in the image.

Storing Images

You can save your image as a file by selecting Get Image.
You can also create a link to your image and send it to your friends by selecting Save & Share.
